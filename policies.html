ISO 9001:2015 is set of standardized requirements for a Quality Management System (QMS). It is designed to help organizations ensure that they meet the needs of customers and other stakeholders while meeting statutory and regulatory requirements related to the product. Quality management means what the organization does to ensure that its products or services satisfy the customer's quality requirements and comply with any regulations applicable to those products or services. It also does to enhance customer satisfaction, and achieve continual improvement of its performance.
